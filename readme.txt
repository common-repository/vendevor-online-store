Vendevor is an easy to use ecommerce plugin and shopping cart widget. Immediately accept credit cards and deposit sales straight to your bank account.

== Description ==

Vendevor is the first all-in-one E-commerce solution that allows you to quickly setup a store on your existing website, blog, Facebook fan page, or Vendevor.com store, upload products from your phone, tablet or computer, and immediately accept credit cards with sales deposited straight to your bank account. Selling online has never been easier.

Learn all about us at vendevor.com.

Check out our company blog where we talk about what it's like growing a tech startup at www.blog.vendevor.com.

== Installation ==

1. Set up an account at vendevor.com
2. On vendevor.com, click the "Publish" tab and select the "Website/Blog" tile
3. Authorize your blog's domain (wordpress.org for example), and then copy the WordPress embed code you wish to use
4. Upload `wp_vendevor.zip` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory on your WordPress site (or install through the WordPress Plugins Directory).
5. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
6. Place the embed code you copied in step 3 in the page you want your Vendevor store to show up and save the page.  Your embed code will look like [vendevor authcode="(code)"]
7. You're done!  Your Vendevor store should now be viewable in your Wordpress site.  All changes you make on Vendevor, including store design changes and product changes, are automatically reflected in your Wordpress site.
